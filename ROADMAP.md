## Version Naming Convention

All versions follow the "stealth/shadow" theme:
- **Major versions** (x.0.0): Mythological/conceptual (Onyx, Eclipse, Phantom)
- **Minor versions** (x.x.0): Shadow-related terms (Shadow, Spectre, Veil)
- **Patch versions** (x.x.x): Incremental fixes

## Release Cycle

- **Major releases**: Every 6-9 months
- **Minor releases**: Every 2-3 months  
- **Patch releases**: As needed for critical bugs
